Tennis Ball Launcher

Dual-wheel launching system — design, prototyping, and testing.

I am working on building a tennis ball launcher powered by dual 775 DC motors and a pair of precisely aligned flywheel shafts mounted on a reinforced wooden base. The goal is to create a consistent, adjustable practice tool for training and for testing projectile motion concepts through hands-on experimentation.
